About

I’m an undergraduate in the Department of Physics at IIT Kanpur. My research is on how learned spectral models — in particular adaptive Fourier neural operators — represent and compress turbulent dynamics, with Bose-Einstein condensates and Kolmogorov turbulence as the cleanest testbeds.

Outside of research I build numerical solvers in C++/CUDA, tinker with LLM-assisted kernel synthesis, and run SimulaQ, a small project trying to make high-end quantum-fluid simulation cheap enough for a physics group to actually use.
